Peg Solitaire

HOW TO PLAY:

  1. Click on a blue peg to select it. Click on an available empty hole to place the selected peg in that hole.
  2. Pegs must jump over one peg and the space they are jumping into must be empty.
  3. When you jump over a peg, that peg is removed from the board.
  4. You win if you can remove all pegs except for one in the middle.
  5. You lose if you run out of moves.
